Recipe for a Simple Dinner

Tofu Bowl Ingrediants

Bowl Ingrediants

  • 1 cup of extra firm Tofu
  • 2 1/2 tbsp cornstarch
  • 2 cups panko crumbs
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper
  • 1/2 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 cup jasmin rice
  • 1 cucumber
  • 1 cup cilantro
  • 1 lime

Sauce Ingrediants

  • 1/2 cup Kinders Teriyaki Sauce
  • 1/3 cup Spicy Chili Sauce

Instructions

  1. Place water in a pot to boil
  2. Cut all vegetables on a cutting board and leave to the side.
  3. Cook rice
  4. Pat down tofu with paper towel to remove extra moisture, place cornstarch and panko crumbs in seperate bowls and cover tofu in cornstrch and panko.
  5. Cook tofu in a non stick pan. Place in bowl to the side once finished
  6. Place rice in a bowl once done cooking and top with vegetables, tofu, and seasonings
  7. Mix together Kinders Teriyaki Sauce and Chili Sauce. Drizzle sauce over bowl

ENJOY!
