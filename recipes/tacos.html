Taco Recipe

Easy to make tacos for Taco Tuesdays!

Ingrediants

  • Street Taco tortillas
  • Salsa
  • Salt and Pepper
  • 1 lb. ground beef
  • Stem of Cilantro
  • 2 Limes

Instructions

  1. Cook the ground beef in a pot. Season with salt and pepper
  2. Once beef has been cooked through, start warming up the tortillas on a griddle
  3. Assemble the tacos on a plate. Place meat on tortillas, pour some of the premade salsa on top with cilantro
  4. Cut up the limes into wedges and place a few on the plate
