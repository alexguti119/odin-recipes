Red Salsa

Great red salsa to add into tacos or as a dip for chips!

Ingrediants

  • 2 lbs. Tomato
  • Cilantro
  • 1 small Onion
  • 2 Jalapeno Peppers
  • 2 cloves of Garlic
  • 1/2 Lime
  • 2 tbsp Olive Oil
  • Seasonings

Instructions

  1. Process all the ingrediants
  2. Heat up a large pot to medium heat with olive oil.
  3. Add in the ingrediants and bring to a quick boil then reduce to a simmer for 15-20 mins
  4. Remove from heat and season with salt if needed and stir
  5. Let the it cool and refrigerate

Add this Salsa to your tacos!
